How to Play

  • Goal: Control the snake to eat food (apples) and grow as long as possible.
  • Controls: Use arrow keys or WASD to change direction. Space to pause, R to restart.
  • Mobile: Use the touch buttons at the bottom of the screen.
  • Game Over: Hitting walls (Wall Mode) or colliding with yourself ends the game.
  • Wrap Mode: Snake exits one side and enters from the opposite side.
